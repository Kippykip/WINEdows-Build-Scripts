Bundled tools and software!

WINEdows comes with essential software right out of the box! Some notable examples include:

WINE - For running Windows software.
LibreOffice - For editing all your MS Word/Excel/Powerpoints documents!
GParted & CloneZilla & QDirStat - An extremely good trio of administration tools for data backup and recovery!
Google Chrome - For all your up to date web browsing needs.
Audacity & Handbrake - For all your audio editing or conversions.
Parole - For watching content or listening to neat soundtracks!

And much much more!
